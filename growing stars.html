About Us

We are dedicated to excellence in education, combining modern learning with future-driven technologies At Growing Stars Nursery and Primary School, our purpose is to nurture every child into becoming a shining star—academically, morally, and spiritually. We are committed to providing a safe, stimulating, and Christ-centered learning environment where children discover their God-given potential and grow with confidence, discipline, and excellence. We believe education goes beyond textbooks; it is about building character, instilling values, and equipping learners with the knowledge and skills needed to thrive in today’s world. Our mission is to partner with parents in raising well-rounded children who will not only excel in their academics but also uphold integrity, creativity, and responsibility in every sphere of life. With passionate teachers, modern teaching methods, and a culture of neatness, punctuality, and hard work, we prepare our learners to stand out as leaders of tomorrow. At Growing Stars, every child is a treasure, every talent is nurtured, and every effort is celebrated.
